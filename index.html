Hey! I am Rahul.

I am a Research Coordinator at the International Growth Centre at the London School of Economics, working for the Firms Initiative. My work sits at the intersection of policy research and project management, covering design, data, operations, and delivery.

I am interested in how inequality, labour markets, and enterprise dynamics shape growth and inclusion.

I have 7+ years of experience coordinating research from design to delivery - survey implementation, data pipelines, budgets, contracts, and multi-country operations.

I trained in Public Policy at University College London and completed the MITx MicroMasters in Statistics and Data Science . I use R, Stata, and Python to clean data, run analysis, and build simple dashboards that turn evidence into clear decisions. I also enjoy writing blogs and policy notes that make research easy to use.

In the past, I have worked with governments and researchers, and earlier spent time as a Gandhi Fellow and at J-PAL South Asia.
